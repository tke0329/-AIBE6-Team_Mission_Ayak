Ibuprofen

NSAID

Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used for treating pain, fever, and inflammation.

healing Primary Effect

Ibuprofen works by reducing hormones that cause inflammation and pain in the body. It is highly effective for mild to moderate pain relief, including headaches, dental pain, menstrual cramps, muscle aches, or arthritis.

schedule Usage Guidelines

  • restaurant

    Take with Food

    Consume with a meal or milk to prevent stomach upset.

  • water_drop

    Hydration

    Take with a full glass of water (8 ounces) unless otherwise directed.

  • update

    Frequency

    Do not take more often than every 4 to 6 hours as needed.

info

Important Precaution

Can increase your risk of fatal heart attack or stroke, especially if you use it long term or take high doses.
